Privacy Protection Checklist
Quick Protection Tips
  • Use a privacy-focused browser (Firefox, Brave) with tracking protection
  • Install an ad/tracker blocker like uBlock Origin
  • Use a VPN to hide your IP address from trackers
  • Regularly clear cookies and browsing data
  • Opt out of data broker sites (many let you request removal)
  • Use encrypted messaging apps (Signal, not SMS)
